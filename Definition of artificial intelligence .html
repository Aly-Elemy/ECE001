Definition of artificial intelligence:-

Industrial intelligence is a software package and not an electrical device, designed to make
the machine able to achieve a specific goal in a way similar to humans or sometimes beyond
the ability of humans. AI consists of a set of different goals and capabilities to include
the ability to learn, organize and understand science

Artificial intelligence (AI): -

the ability of a digital computer or computer-controlled robot to
perform tasks commonly associated with intelligent beings. The term is frequently applied to the
project of developing systems endowed with the intellectual processes characteristic of humans,
such as the ability to reason, discover meaning, generalize, or learn from past experience. Since
the development of the digital computer in the 1940s, it has been demonstrated that computers
can be programmed to carry out very complex tasks there are as yet no programs that can match
human flexibility over wider domains or in tasks requiring much everyday knowledge. On the
other hand, some programs have attained the performance levels of human experts and professionals
in performing certain specific tasks, so that artificial intelligence in this limited sense is
found in applications as diverse as medical diagnosis, computer search engines, and voice or handwriting
recognition.
